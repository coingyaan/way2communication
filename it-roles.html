Software Engineering Roles

Frontend Developers

Specialists in modern frontend frameworks and technologies, focused on building responsive and scalable user interfaces.

Backend Developers

Engineers experienced in server-side technologies, APIs, databases, and system architecture to support high-performance applications.

Full Stack Developers

Professionals capable of handling both frontend and backend development across complete application lifecycles.

Cloud & DevOps Roles

Cloud Engineers

Experts in cloud platforms such as AWS, Azure, and Google Cloud, supporting scalable infrastructure and cloud-native solutions.

DevOps Engineers

Professionals focused on CI/CD pipelines, automation, infrastructure as code, and improving deployment reliability.

Site Reliability Engineers (SRE)

Specialists ensuring system availability, performance, and scalability through monitoring and reliability engineering practices.

Data, AI & Analytics Roles

Data Engineers

Professionals responsible for building and maintaining data pipelines, warehouses, and analytics infrastructure.

Data Analysts

Analysts skilled in transforming data into actionable insights to support business decision-making.

Data Scientists

Experts in machine learning, statistical modeling, and advanced analytics to solve complex business problems.

QA & Cybersecurity Roles

QA & Automation Engineers

Specialists focused on software quality assurance, test automation, and ensuring reliable application performance.

Cybersecurity Specialists

Professionals responsible for securing systems, networks, and applications against modern security threats.

Application Security Engineers

Experts in identifying and mitigating security vulnerabilities within software applications.

Technology Leadership Roles

Tech Leads

Senior engineers who guide technical direction, mentor teams, and ensure high-quality delivery.

Engineering Managers

Leaders responsible for managing engineering teams, delivery timelines, and cross-functional collaboration.

CTO & Senior Technology Leaders

Executive-level professionals responsible for defining technology vision, strategy, and long-term innovation.
