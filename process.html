Our IT Recruitment Process

Our recruitment process is designed to ensure quality, speed, and accuracy. We follow a structured methodology that helps organizations hire the right technology talent with minimal risk and maximum efficiency.

1. Requirement Analysis

We begin by understanding your business objectives, technical requirements, team structure, and hiring timelines to ensure complete clarity.

2. Talent Sourcing

Using targeted sourcing strategies, we identify suitable candidates from our network, job portals, referrals, and direct outreach.

3. Technical Screening

Candidates undergo structured technical and behavioral screening to evaluate skills, experience, and role alignment.

4. Client Interviews

Shortlisted profiles are shared with you, and we coordinate interviews while managing feedback and follow-ups.

5. Offer & Onboarding Support

We assist with offer negotiation, candidate engagement, documentation, and onboarding to ensure successful joining.

6. Post-Placement Follow-up

Our engagement continues post-joining to ensure satisfaction for both the client and the candidate.
